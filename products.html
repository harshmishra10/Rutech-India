Our Products

Very wide range of Exothermic / Insulating Sleeves in various shapes and sizes are manufactured having different "M.E.F.", from 1.3 to 1.8 as per requirement. Rutech's Feeding System products provide: • Improved yield, by dramatically reducing the amount of feed metal required to produce a sound casting through the consistent thermal performance of the products. • Reduced defects, by providing consistent feed performance where required in castings, eliminating "unavoidable" shrink-related defects inherent in some component designs. • Increased productivity, because smaller risers may make room for more castings per pattern or flask, thereby increasing foundry productivity in large increments. Small risers and attached breaker cores also reduce the contact area that must be cleaned, leading to additional productivity improvements. • Improved quality, because the superior metallurgical and physical properties of properly fed castings improve the overall quality of the finished product.
