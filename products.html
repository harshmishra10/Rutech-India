Very wide range of Exothermic / Insulating Sleeves in various shapes and sizes are manufactured having different "M.E.F.", from 1.3 to 1.8 as per requirement.

Rutech's Feeding System products provide:

  • Improved yield, by dramatically reducing the amount of feed metal required to produce a sound casting through the consistent thermal performance of the products.
  • Reduced defects, by providing consistent feed performance where required in castings, eliminating "unavoidable" shrink-related defects inherent in some component designs.
  • Increased productivity, because smaller risers may make room for more castings per pattern or flask, thereby increasing foundry productivity in large increments. Small risers and attached breaker cores also reduce the contact area that must be cleaned, leading to additional productivity improvements.
  • Improved quality, because the superior metallurgical and physical properties of properly fed castings improve the overall quality of the finished product.

Properties obtained will be –

  1. RI Top is light in weight, robust and flexible and lending itself ideally to use in all sizes of molds as per requirement.
  2. Reduces undesirable segregation of impurities and increases the yield of sound steel in the final ingot.
  3. Influences the amount and position of cavitation by reducing it to a minimum and locating it where it will be the least harmful in the solidified ingot.

Fluxes play a very important role in Iron & Steel Industries. The fluxes are produced to match the requirements of the iron & steel industry to obtain maximum benefits.

Rutech's CoverExo-S hot toppings are exothermic / insulating, low-density, efficient riser toppings, providing good flowability, good expansion, and good heat retention. Rutech's riser topping compounds spread quickly and evenly over the entire molten metal surface, without raking. Little smoke is generated during the exothermic reaction as the compound expands to at least twice its original thickness.

The resulting highly efficient insulating cover remains soft and non-cracking, maintaining maximum insulating value as it follows the head metal down.

  • Excellent flowability for complete coverage
  • Excellent insulation properties
  • High energy heat output minimizes chill
  • Consistency of performance
  • Ease of use
  • Economy of application

The main fluxes produced are as follows:

  1. COVEREXO-S – Exothermic Anti-Piping Compound used in Risers (Swelling Type).
  2. THERMOFORM – Exothermic Compound (Minimum 35–40% metal yield).
  3. COREX – Ready-made insulating base gum in 2 kg bottles (A Core-Mould Joining Compound).
  4. FERROTEEM – Bottom pouring / teeming compound for ingot mould castings.
