About Rutech India

Founded in 1998 by Shri N. R. Misra, RUTECH INDIA is one of the leading manufacturers of feeding aids (Foundry Fluxes and Chemicals) for Foundries (Ferrous & Non-Ferrous). Right from the beginning, we never compromised with quality and hence, quality has always been the main motto of RUTECH since its inception. Advanced production techniques are employed in process control, all backed by a full range of laboratory testing to ensure perfect and economical products.

The company banks on its expertise, built through experience in this industry over more than a decade. Presently, we are operating from Bhilai. The company will be extremely glad to support your casting processes and is always ready to conduct trials at your workshop. We believe in a symbiotic relationship with our customers, sincerely understand customer values, and are committed to excellence.
